Shelter over All

You imbue the builders of the village with basic woodcutting and building techniques that allow them to create simple huts. They use strong slices of wood from the nearby forests and sharp rocks to create axes. They set out to chop trees to gather wood. After many hours of work they have created a new house in the center of the village that is comprised of logs with indents cut in the ends to stack on one another. There appears to be unrest between the members however over who gets to live in the cabin.
