Revolution!

The sect of the village that was unhappy with the system in place makes its move during the night. They abduct the leaders of the village and leave them deep in the forest with their arms tied and blindfolded. When they return to the village they take control of the political system. In fear of others looking to change the system and asking you for your help, they shun you from the village and destroy all record of your ever having been there.
