Council!

The villagers ask you how to create a council where people can propose ideas and vote for a decision. After explaining the basic concept to the village they appear to understand it much better than you had anticipated. They quickly adopt the system and begin to make changes toward improving the village. They create new technologies that you didn't know they were capable of through experimentation and sharing of thoughts during council. They are more than cappable of surviving now and you feel as if your work here is done.
