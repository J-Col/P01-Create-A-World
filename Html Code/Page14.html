Cattle over Crops!

The storm hits the village in the night. While everyone is asleep, the rains pour down onto the fields. The cattle have been moved into one of the houses for the night in an attempt to save them. The fields flood and wash away all the crops from the village. The following day the villagers are forced to slaughter a cow for food. It appears that the cow was infected with mad cow disease, and within a few months time the entire village has fallen ill. Three months later, only one villager remains, he damns you for your choice and dies later that night.
