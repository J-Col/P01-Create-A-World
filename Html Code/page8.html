Animals!

The large field of vegetation has attracted a herd of cattle to the settlement. The villagers managed to trap them but don't know what to with them. You give them the most basic understanding of raising livestock in an attempt to further their developmnet. This new knowledge drives them to create pens for the cows and herd them inside, away from their farm lands. A strong storm is approaching, the villagers look to you for guidance. Do they save the cattle or the crops?
